Come as you are. Leave as you want to be.

Welcome to Jamestown Community College! We are eager to share with you all that JCC has to offer at our locations in Jamestown, Olean, and Dunkirk.

At JCC, you can take courses to graduate job-ready or transfer into bachelor's degree programs. JCC also offers dual enrollment opportunities for high school students, applied learning possibilities, customized training for business and industry, and workforce readiness programs. JCC faculty and staff bring a wealth of knowledge and experience, and care deeply about your success.

Our learning spaces, from smart classrooms to science labs and music studios to libraries, are exceptional. It's easy to get involved in student clubs, NJCAA athletics, and more.

We strive to be on the cutting edge of learning by intently listening to our partners and exploring new, innovative ways to better meet the needs of the communities we serve in southwestern New York. Check out JCC’s social media channels for updates as JCC adds new in-demand programs and services.

Come as you are. Leave as you want to be.

Sincerely,

Daniel DeMarte, Ed.D.
President of Jamestown Community College
